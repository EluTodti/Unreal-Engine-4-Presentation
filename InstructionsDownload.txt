Step 1: 
go to
https://accounts.unrealengine.com/register?lang=en_US&redirectUrl=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.unrealengine.com%2Fdownload%3Fdismiss%3Dhttps%253A%252F%252Fwww.unrealengine.com%252F&client_id=43e2dea89b054198a703f6199bee6d5b&noHostRedirect=true
and create an Epic Games account

Step 2:
Download and install the Epic Games Launcher

Step 3:
(Log in)
In the Epic Games Launcher click on the tab "Unreal Engine" and download the Unreal Engine (on the left)

And finally:
Create a project via Blueprint or c++ - your choice. (For the demo I will simply use a blueprint)


If you are interested, watch these video tutorials about the basics of the Unreal Engine
https://docs.unrealengine.com/latest/INT/Videos/
or visit the Unreal Engine Community Wiki
https://wiki.unrealengine.com/Main_Page



I recommend to bring a mouse ;)


Camera movement:
Right-klick and hold to look around in every direction.
Left-klick and hold to look around in the x/y - axis. Move the mouse forward / backward to do so with the camera.
Right- and left-klick and hold to move on the x axis. Move the mouse forward / backward to move on the z axis.
